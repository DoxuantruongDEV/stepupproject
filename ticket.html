Explore more of Nexus Aquarium

VinKE & Vinpearl Aquarium Times City is the place that attracts the most tourists in Times City. With an area of nearly 4,000 square meters, this place is like a miniature underground ocean. Vinpearl Aquarium has a capacity of 3 million cubic meters of sea water and is home to more than 30,000 marine creatures As the first aquarium with a tunnel in Hanoi, Vinpearl Aquarium includes 3 subdivisions with their own ecological floors:

1.1. Freshwater area - like the primeval forest of the Amazon: Visitors visiting the freshwater area will feel like they are walking around the Amazon primeval forest. Scenes that seem to only appear in movies have now been faithfully recreated in this tourist area. Tourists will be able to directly admire freshwater fish such as blood dragon fish, stingrays, red-tailed king snapper, elephant seal fish, freshwater white shark,...

1.2. Reptile cave area - many unique reptiles: Through the tropical jungle in the freshwater area, visitors will enter the cave area to witness firsthand the life of reptiles and amphibians such as such as golden pythons, lizards, lizards, lizards, earth dragons, crocodiles, etc. In addition, in the cave there are also many familiar insects of tropical ecosystems such as rose spiders and scorpions. Emperor,...

1.3. Saltwater area - eye-catching underwater performances: Saltwater area is a destination that attracts a large number of visitors, especially those who always wonder what Times City has to offer. In addition to the diverse marine life system (corals, webbed rays, devil-faced fish, grouper fish, knifefish, lionfish,...), the saltwater fish area is also the place where many events take place. Attractive items such as mermaid performances, fish feeding shows,... In particular, Vinpearl Aquarium is also the first place in Vietnam to have penguins. Visitors will not need to set foot in the distant Antarctica but can admire the penguins right here.
